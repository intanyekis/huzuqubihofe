After hosting the Canadian reality TV show The Hills for many years, Dan Levy quit the job and began working as a writer for small projects. Then the artist came up with a story of a wealthy family who lost everything. Once his father Eugene Levy came on board, the duo brought a reluctant Catherine O’Hara on board, who went on to stay for the entirety of the show. Schitt’s Creek is a story about the Rose family who goes overnight bankrupt and moves to the small town of Schitt’s Creek, which was their only remaining asset. The television series went on to have a clean sweep at the 2021 Emmys, winning every major category of the season.

Despite the show ending in 2020, the popularity of Schitt’s Creek has only grown with every passing year, and the cast has become household names for their eccentric personalities on screen. Let’s look at Schitt’s Creek’s cast, ranked by net worth.

Jennifer Robertson ($2.5 million)

Jennifer Robertson portrays the lovable yet strong-minded character of Jocelyn Schitt, a high school English teacher and the mayor’s wife. Robertson, whose father was a comedy writer, knew that she wanted to pursue being an actress from a young age. The actress has several acting credits, including Wingin’ It and Ginny & Georgia.

Emily Hampshire ($3 million)

Making her debut at 15, Emily Hampshire has more than 80 acting roles in movies and television shows. She portrayed the role of Stevie Budd, the receptionist/owner of the motel and best friend of Levy’s character David. Her notable projects include Rookie Blue, 12 Monkeys, and Mother! She won seven Canadian Screen Awards for her performance, as noted by Celebrity Net Worth.

Sarah Levy ($4 million)

Actress and singer Sarah Levy showed off her singing chops in the show as a part of a female choir group Jazzagals in Schitt’s Creek. Sister of Daniel Levy, Sarah, got cast as the waitress Twyla Sands early on during pre-production. The actress has worked with notable actors like Julia Roberts and Tom Hanks in Larry Crowne and became a series regular in 2020 for the show SurrealEstate.

Noah Reid ($4 million)

Another musician on the list, Noah Reid, came on board during the third season of the show as a co-owner and love interest of David Rose, Patrick Brewer. His character became an instant fan favorite among the audiences, and Reid gained worldwide recognition. Reid has continued to focus on his music career and made his Broadway debut in 2022 with The Minutes.

Dustin Milligan ($4 million)

Dustin Milligan first became famous for playing a supporting role in CW’s drama show 90210. In Schitt’s Creek, Milligan portrayed the role of Theodore ‘Ted’ Mullens, a veterinary and love interest of Alexis Rose, opposite Annie Murphy. After the show, the actor starred in another comedy sitcom Rutherford Falls, produced by NBC’s Peacock net worth.

Annie Murphy ($4 million)

While Annie Murphy had small stints in television and movies, it wasn’t until she was cast as the bold and charming Alexis Rose that the actress shot to fame. An Emmy-winning role, Alexis is known for her creative problem-solving and unbelievable adventure stories from her youth. According to IMDB, Murphy has added several credits to her, starring in Russian Doll, Kevin Can F Himself, and Fairfax.

Chris Elliott ($10 million)

Roland Schitt, the town’s mayor, became an instant favorite for fans of the show, and with Chris Elliott’s comedic timing and genius, he is an unforgettable character. Elliott is one of the most recognized Canadian comedy actors known for his notable work in How I Met Your Mother, Everybody Loves Raymond, Scary Movie 2, and Groundhog Day.

Catherine O’Hara ($10 million)

Considered to be one of the best comedians in Hollywood, Catherine O’Hara brings a wide range of skills to every role she portrays. As stated by Screen Rant, the actress has given critically acclaimed performances in Home Alone, Best In Show, and Beetlejuice. In Schitt’s Creek, O’Hara plays the lead role of Moira Rosa, a former daily soap actress who talks with various accents, earning her an Emmy award.

Daniel Levy ($14 million)

An actor, writer, screenwriter, producer, and co-creator of Schitt’s Creek, Daniel Levy is the genius behind bringing Schitt’s Creek to the screen. Playing the role of David Rose, Levy broke many stereotypes about how same-sex relationships are portrayed on TV. Since the show, Levy has been busy on several projects, including Happiest Season, The Idol, and will be next seen in Sex Education season three.

Eugene Levy ($20 million)

Eugene Levy, co-creator of the show, also played Dan’s father, Johnny Rose. According to Yahoo Finance, Levy is most notably known for his supporting role in American Pie. The actor has given many comedic gems throughout his career, with some of his best performances starring alongside longtime friend Catherine O’Hara.

Other notable supporting characters in the show include Karen Robinson, Tim Rozon, and Rizwan Manji. With stories showcasing a town with no stigma about homosexuality, Dan and Eugene Levy created a show about love and comedy while broaching serious topics often overlooked by others.
