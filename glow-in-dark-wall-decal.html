Why Should You Consider Buying Glow in Dark Wall Stickers?

If you have been debating whether you should get the glow-in-dark wall decal for a room in your house, here are some reasons why you should go ahead and give them a shot.

Decor element

Glow in the dark decals and stickers come in various attractive shapes, sizes, and colors. They serve as outstanding decor elements and instantly enhance the visual appeal of any room. The children’s playroom, sleeping room, and study area particularly benefit from this attractive yet effortless addition to the space.

Nighttime utility

Many children and adults fear complete darkness but sleeping in bright light have counterproductive impacts on overall health as it can potentially disturb circadian rhythm, among other factors. Thus, using glow-in-the-dark wall decals in rooms of adults who fear pitch darkness can keep the fears at bay and create a comfortable and relaxing environment.

Learning

If chosen carefully, the glow-in-the-dark wall decal can prove to be instrumental for learning. With the growing variety of such decals available in the market, many have an educational nature and help children learn in a fun manner.

Environmentally friendly

Glow in the dark wall decals does not require power or take up any additional charging. There is no recharge energy and equipment involved as well. Thus, glow-in-the-dark wall decals are great for the environment, particularly in comparison to night lights.

What Things Should You Keep In Mind When Purchasing Glow in Dark Wall Decals?

Material

The glow-in-the-dark wall decals are vinyl PVC, paper, and plastic. Depending on your choice, the paper decals are two-dimensional, and the three-dimensional look is achieved by a combination of vinyl PVC and plastic. Moreover, the paper has an inherent sticker back while the others are stuck with adhesive and putty.

Size

Glow in the dark wall decals are available in various sizes. It is advisable to choose the size that fits adequately on the wall and ceiling where you plan to adhere them. The dimensions in the description must be cross-checked to ensure optimal-sized products.

Design

Formerly, glow-in-the-dark wall decals were only available in star and galaxy shapes. However, now the sky's the limit to glow-in-the-dark wall decal designs—more complex solar systems, galaxies, constellations, animals, birds, natural scenes, and letters. The designs are either available in two-dimensional or three-dimensional. Choose the design that complements the existing theme of your room for achieving a cohesive look.

Color

Glow in the dark wall decals are available in two varieties, a unicolor one and the other with a full-spectrum color range. The choice depends on the interior requirements and the budget. The unicolor ones have a soft glow and come in yellow, green, pink, and blue. However, the colored ones are on the higher end.

Customer reviews

The customer reviews under the glow in the wall decal of choice should be thoroughly read to determine the utility and yield of the product. As a rule of thumb, the product with the highest customer rating should be purchased to benefit from others’ experiences and avoid misfortunes.

Price of Glow In The Dark Wall Decals

Glow in the dark wall decals have different price ranges depending on the complexity of design, material, and size of the decal. The smaller, less complex structures can be purchased for less than $10. However, the ones available under $10 are mostly the stick-on and molded variety with poor sticking and tend to fall off or become dull with time.

The detailed and educational designs are usually available in medium to large sizes. These are generally high-quality designs and have better sticking and glowing properties. The glow in the dark wall decals of this kind lies in the range of $10 to $25, with the more complex designs generally priced higher.

Given the price point, purchasing the cheaper ones can be tempting, but buying the ones with a maximum educational benefit is essential, which can be a bit on the pricey side.

How to Make the Most Out of Your Glow in the Dark Wall Decal?

Here are a few tips on how to make the most out of the glow in the dark wall decal with minimal effort:

  • Leaving the glow in the dark wall decal out in the sun for a few hours before fixing them in place on the wall or ceiling will only enhance their illumination.
  • The glow-in-dark wall decal absorbs illumination from other light sources, and manually charging is both exhaustive and bothersome. It is best to place these in a brightly lit room with adequate sunlight or place a light source near the wall where it has been applied.
  • Rental homes require a lot of extra caution when sticking anything on the wall, so it is better to purchase the molded stick-on instead of decals.
  • It is tricky to use glow-in-the-dark wall decals on textured surfaces.
  • Before sticking them decals and stickers, it is advised to check the placement.
  • Clean the sticking surface before applying the wall decal, so there is better adaptation and close contact between the adherent and the adherend.
  • The decals can be easily removed. If not, a hairdryer can be used to loosen the sticking.
  • Glow in the dark wall decals can also be used in various art and crafts projects.
