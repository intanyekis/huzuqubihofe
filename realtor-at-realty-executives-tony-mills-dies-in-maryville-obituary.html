Realtor at Realty Executives Tony Mills dies in Maryville: Obituary

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  308 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The community of West Maryville is mourning the loss of David Anthony Mills, a beloved resident, former teacher, and esteemed Realtor at Realty Executives Associates Maryville. The tragic news of his passing has left a void in the hearts of those who knew him.

Announced through a poignant social media post, the message reads, “Please say some prayers to this amazing family who has lost a very special former teacher and father. Tony Mills, you will be missed dearly! All the kids loved you.”

David Anthony Mills, known as Tony, was a native of Alcoa, Tennessee, and attended Alcoa High School from 1979 to 1983.
He pursued his passion for education at Middle Tennessee State University, earning a Bachelor of Applied Science in Elementary Education and Teaching from 1984 to 1988. Tony dedicated eight years to Friendsville Elementary School, serving as a Teacher from 1991 to 1999.

He exemplified dedication as a Logistics First Sergeant at Tennessee Air National Guard from 1986 to 2006.

In 2011, Tony transitioned to a career in real estate, choosing Realty Executives for its national presence. He thrived as a Real Estate Agent, also venturing into property management, assisting homeowners with care and diligence.
A member of Broadway Baptist Church, Tony actively contributed to his community through volunteering.

Tony Mills was not only a dedicated professional but also a loving father to his children. Known for his genuine and kind-hearted nature, Tony made a positive impact on everyone he encountered.

Details about Tony Mills’ obituary and funeral arrangements will be shared by the family at a later date, allowing the community to come together to honor and remember a remarkable individual.

In this time of grief, the community extends its deepest condolences to the family and friends of David Anthony Mills, cherishing the memories of a man who touched the lives of many.
