Christopher Michael Pratt, popularly known as Chris Pratt, has become an acclaimed name in Hollywood. Born on June 21, 1979, in Virginia, Minnesota, Pratt has carved a name for himself with his outstanding performances in blockbuster franchises like Jurassic World and Guardians of the Galaxy. From his humble beginnings as a waiter in Maui, Hawaii, Pratt has climbed the ladder of success to amass a staggering net worth of $90 million. This article delves into how Chris Pratt earns and spends his jaw-dropping fortune.

Chris Pratt’s astounding net worth generated from multiple sources has allowed him to indulge in a lavish lifestyle. From luxury cars and boisterous mansions to philanthropic endeavors, Chris knows how to spend his millions.

Earns - Outstanding Acting Career

Chris Pratt's acting career forms the backbone of his massive net worth. His breakthrough role came in 2009 with NBC's Parks and Recreation, and he later attained international stardom in 2014 after starring in MCU’s Guardian of the Galaxy. Since then, there has been no looking back, and Chris has become one of the most bankable stars in the industry, bagging role after role in blockbuster movies. His salary has been upward since his Guardians of the Galaxy role. According to Cosmopolitan, Chris made $26 Million in 2016 alone, starring in movies like Passengers and Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2. He added $10 Million to his net worth by reprising his role in Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom. Chris Pratt’s current salaries include an astounding $15-$20 Million for Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 and $12-$14 million for Jurassic World Dominion.

Spends - Extravagant Engagement Ring

Pratt's personal life also reflects his financial success. In 2018 he proposed to Katherine Schwarzenegger, daughter of Hollywood stalwart Arnold Schwarzenegger. He did so with a five-carat diamond ring that reportedly cost between $150,000 and $550,000, as per reports by The Insider. This extravagant purchase provided a peek into Pratt's financial prowess.

Earns - Career As A Producer

In addition to acting, Chris Pratt has also ventured into production. He has served as an executive producer and producer for several movies and TV shows. Pratt’s first production venture came in the form of the 2012 sports documentary titled On The Mat. His other production ventures include big-budget action movies and TV shows like The Tomorrow War and The Terminal List, in which he also co-starred. These dual roles increased Pratt’s creative control over the project and added to his earnings, contributing to his impressive net worth.

Spends - Boisterous Real Estate

Chris Pratt's real estate investments include multiple lavish mansions. His many properties have a $15.6 million mansion in the Los Angeles Pacific Palisades neighborhood. This luxurious property spans 10,000 square feet and boasts a gym, a movie theater, and a wine cellar, among other luxury amenities. Pratt and his ex-wife Anna Farris used to own a $3.3 million home in the Hollywood Hills which boasted a 4,700-square-foot living space, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Earns - Brand Endorsements

Brand endorsements form another significant part of Chris Pratt's earnings. He has endorsed major brands like Michelob ULTRA, an American beer brand, and Bridgestone, a multinational auto and truck parts manufacturer. In 2021, Golf and Lifestyle Brand, TravisMathew roped in Chris Pratt as its brand ambassador. These endorsements increase Pratt’s visibility and add a substantial amount to his net worth.

Spends - Charity And Philanthropy

Over the years, Chris Pratt has proved himself to be an avid philanthropist with multiple donations for the welfare of the downtrodden. In 2015, Pratt and his ex-wife Anna Farris donated $1 million to provide eyeglasses to underprivileged children. Further, in 2016, he donated $500,000 to a teen center in Lake Stevens named after his father. Pratt’s recent donations include $100,000 to the Feed Thy Neighbor charity and $10,000 to Edmonds Food Bank.

Earns - Appearances In TVCs And Video Games

Chris Pratt's earnings also come from his appearances in television commercials and lending his voice in video games. He has appeared in TV commercials for brands like Michelob and McDonald's. Pratt has lent his voice to characters in video games like The Lego Movie Videogame and Lego Jurassic World. These ventures provide an additional income stream, contributing to his overall net worth.

Spends - Swanky Car Collection

Pratt's love for cars is evident in his impressive car collection. He owns a range of luxury vehicles, including a $150,000 BMW i8, a $125,000 Range Rover Sport, and a $111,000 Tesla Model S, to name a few, as per 21 Motoring. These high-end vehicles showcase Pratt's love for luxury and willingness to spend on his passions.

Chris Pratt's $90 million net worth is a consequence of his successful career in Hollywood and his savvy financial decisions. From his earnings as an actor and producer to his brand endorsements and appearances in commercials and video games, Pratt has multiple income streams. At the same time, he enjoys his wealth, spending on real estate, luxury cars, and personal milestones while giving back to the community through charitable donations.
