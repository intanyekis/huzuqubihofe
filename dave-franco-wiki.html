Dave Franco Wiki/Bio

David John Franco was born on June 12, 1985, in Palo Alto, California, USA. He is a professional American actor, voice actor, and filmmaker. He graduated from Palo Alto High School in 2003 and then enrolled at the University of Southern California. Late Douglas Eugene Franco (1948–2011) is his father's name, and Betsy Lou is his mother's. He made his directorial debut in 2020 with The Rental, starring his wife Alison Brie. He made his acting debut in 2006 on The CW drama television series 7th Heaven.

Dave Franco Wiki

David John Franco is an actor, voice actor, and filmmaker of American origin. He began his career with minor roles in films like Superbad (2007) and Charlie St. Cloud (2010). He has also appeared in the films Fright Night (2011), Now You See Me (2013), and its sequel Now You See Me 2 (2016), Neighbors (2014), Nerve (2016), and The Disaster Artist (2017). In 2018, he co-starred in Netflix's addiction drama 6 Balloons with Abbi Jacobson.

Franco made his directorial debut in 2020 with the horror-thriller The Rental, based on a screenplay he co-wrote with Joe Swanberg and starring Alison Brie, Dan Stevens, Jeremy Allen White, and Sheila Vand.
