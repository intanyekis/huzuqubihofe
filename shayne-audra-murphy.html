Being a child of infamous comedian and actor, Eddie Murphy, Shayne was born into a star-studded household. She is a model by profession like her sisters.

Shayne was born on October 10, 1994, in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America to parents Eddie Murphy and Nicole Mitchell Murphy. She was named Shayne Audra Murphy at birth. She finished her school from Wildwood School and completed her graduation from Loyola Marymount College. 

Relationship Status: Single

The 25-year-old is currently not dating anyone. Her past relationships are not disclosed. She values her private life to be private and doesn't indulge in sharing.

Shayne's father, Eddie, however, is known to have very public relationships.

How Shayne's mother and father met?

Shayne's father and mother first met at NAACP Image Awards in 1988. The pair would fall for each other head over heels. They dated for two years and then it was the right time to get married. They were married on 18th March 1993, at the Grand Ballroom of The Plaza Hotel in New York City.

The couple was at that point inseparable, and it seemed they were finally settled. They had five children together Bria, Myles, Shayne, Zola, and Bella. But things would not work out for them. In August of 2005, Shayne's mom, Nicole, filed for divorce. The divorce was finalized in April of 2006 when Shyane was just 11 years old.
