YFN Lucci is a rapper and songwriter from the United States. Lucci’s debut mixtape, “Wish Me Well,” is his most well-known work (2014). “Key to the Streets,” “Everyday We Lit,” and “I Wonder Why” are some of his most well-known hit tunes. YFN Lucci’s Early Life: Birth Date/ Parents/ Education?

YFN Lucci was born in Atlanta, Georgia, United States, on February 16, 1991. Rayshawn Lamar Bennett is his given name. He is an American citizen. His zodiac sign is Aquarius. He was born in Kingston, Jamaica, to Jamaican parents.

Lucci grew up in the Summer Hill area. He has an older brother, YFN Kay, who is a rapper as well.

Southside High School was his alma mater. He was a part of the football squad in his high school.

Tupac Shakur, Lil Wayne, Ja Rule, Hot Boys, Jay-Z, The Diplomats, T.I., Young Jeezy, and Fabolous were among his childhood idols. After being motivated by the rapper Johnny Cinco and his older brother, he chose to pursue his gift as a career at the age of 16.

YFN Lucci’s Career: Music Albums?

  •  YFN Lucci kicked off his career after releasing two records off of Cinco’s “John Popi” mixtape in 2014.
  • He signed a record deal with “Think It’s A Game Entertainment” and released his debut mixtape, “Wish Me Well” in December 2015.
  • He released the “Wish Me Well 2” mixtape in 2016, which reached number 183 on the US Billboard 200 chart.
  • His other single, “Key to the Streets” was included in XXL magazine’s “50 Best Hip-Hop Songs” and Vibe magazine’s “The 60 Best Songs” Of 2016 list.
  • He also was featured on the cover of Rolling Out magazine’s “Hidden Hip-Hop Gems of Summer 16” on September 2016.
  • He was included in Billboard magazine’s 10 Hip-Hop in 2016 and R&B Artists to Watch In 2017 list.
  • Lucci released his debut extended play, “Long Live Nut” in April 2017 that debuted at number 27 on the Billboard 200.
  • His single, “Everyday we Lit” of EP was an instant hit.
  • He released his first studio album entitled “Ray Ray From Summerhill” on March 9, 2018.
  • He was chosen along with artist Jacquees to star in Sean John’s holiday 2017 and spring 2018.
  • In 2019, he released the Gangsta Grillz mixtape “650Luc”, which included a Trey Songz-assisted single, “All Night Long.”
