Tippi Toes Founders

Tippi Toes was founded by Sarah Nuse and Megan Reilly in 1999. With a shared passion for dance and a vision to make it accessible to children of all ages and abilities, they embarked on an incredible journey that led to the creation of Tippi Toes. As of 2023, both founders have a net worth of $2.7 million.

Sarah Nuse, in addition to her work with Tippi Toes, is a talented podcaster and author of the inspiring book “Destined for Greatness.” Her dedication to empowering others shines through her various endeavors.

Megan Reilly, on the other hand, is a member of the prestigious Talent Champions Council. Her extensive experience in the dance industry and deep understanding of children’s development have been instrumental in shaping Tippi Toes into the successful program it is today.

Together, Sarah Nuse and Megan Reilly have not only established Tippi Toes as a renowned provider of children’s dance classes, but they have also inspired countless young dancers to find their rhythm, embrace their creativity, and build confidence through the art of dance.

Tippi Toes Franchise Requirements

If you’re considering becoming a Tippi Toes franchisee, there are certain requirements you should be aware of. These requirements help ensure that franchisees have the necessary financial resources to successfully operate a Tippi Toes dance program business.

Financial Requirements

In order to qualify as a Tippi Toes franchisee, you must have a minimum liquid capital of $75,000 and a minimum net worth of $150,000. This helps to ensure that you have the financial stability to support the initial investment and ongoing operations of your franchise.

Total Investment

The total investment for a Tippi Toes franchise ranges from $67,100 to $83,500. This includes the franchise fee of $49,500, which grants you access to the Tippi Toes brand, business model, and ongoing support.

Ongoing Support and Training

As a Tippi Toes franchisee, you can expect to receive ongoing support and training from the Tippi Toes team. This includes assistance with marketing and advertising, operations guidance, and access to a network of fellow franchisees. You’ll have the opportunity to learn from the experience and expertise of others in the Tippi Toes community.
