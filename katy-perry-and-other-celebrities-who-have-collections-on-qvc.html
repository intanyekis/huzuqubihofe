Katy Perry &amp; Other Celebrities Who Have Collections On QVC

 Posted on June 15, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1205 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Have you ever been watching television and flipping through the channels when you see one of your favorite celebrities on QVC? These days, more and more celebrities are taking their products to the television, and promoting them on QVC where their devoted fans and QVC fans alike can shop until they drop, making for the perfect combination to let their businesses thrive.

Selling on QVC and television, in general, might seem like a thing of the past, but many celebrities have been turning to television and finding a lot of success. Many stars have gone on to create empires and expand their brands, all thanks to viewers watching and wanting to spend their money. QVC, a thing of the past, is now the way of the future.

9 Katy Perry

Katy Perry is known for her eclectic fashion choices, and she has taken her creativity and love for fashion to create her very own line of shoes. The Katy Perry Footwear Collection features shoes for any occasion with Katy's very own personal touch on them. All of the shoe styles are modern and definitely will get people talking. Katy has shown her collection on QVC many times and has even appeared on the channel herself to promote the line and help sell her collection which is very popular with fans and shoe lovers everywhere.

8 RuPaul

Being the world's most famous drag queen, RuPaul Charles definitely has an eye for makeup. Because of this, fans went wild when they found out that he would be collaborating with Mally Beauty on a makeup collection — Mally X RuPaul Color Cosmetics Collection.

The makeup like is exclusively available on QVC, and RuPaul even appeared on QVC to help sell and promote the products in the collection. Even though Ru is known for his over-the-top makeup whenever he's in drag, he made sure to make makeup that was for everyone, using a lot of nude and natural tones so everyone from the everyday person to drag queens can use the makeup.

7 Martha Stewart

We all know that Martha Stewart is an amazing businesswoman as she has essentially built her very own empire all by herself. Martha is one of many celebrities who is also utilizing QVC to help promote and sell her products. Martha has her very own collection that is featured on QVC and sells everything from clothing to home and decor, as well as things for the kitchen and for cooking, of course. Martha has hundreds of products that are featured on QVC that will help decorate your home for any occasion, help you cook your favorite meal, and even help you look your best.

6 The Property Brothers

The Property Brothers, also known as Jonathan and Drew Scott have built their own empire all thanks to their HGTV show. Just like Martha Stewart, the brothers also utilize QVC to help sell and promote their own products. The two brothers launched their own line, called Scott Living, which features a number of products to help decorate your home and backyard the way that they do on their shows.

The line primarily focuses on bringing indoor living outdoors, and the collection is exclusively featured on QVC. Fans of the show are able to decorate their homes just like the brothers would do on television. It's like having a home renovation from the Scott brothers without actually having one.

5 Rachael Ray

Rachael Ray is known for making her 30 Minute Meals, and showing you how to cook some tasty dishes on her own talk show. Fans of her shows know that Rachael uses a number of her own products whenever she's cooking, making fans want to have a piece of Rachael in their kitchen as well. As a result, Rachael teamed up with QVC to have her Rachael Ray collection featured. Of course, her products focus on cooking, and she sells cookware, bakeware, and other kitchen tools to help you whip up a meal in your very own kitchen just like Rachael does.

4 Lisa Rinna

Of course, Lisa Rinna is known for being a Real Housewife of Beverly Hills, but you might not know that she also has her very own collection that is sold on QVC. Her clothing brand, the Lissa Rinna Collection has sold millions on QVC and has really made her stand out in the fashion industry. QVC first approached Lisa back in 2011 when they stopped in her clothing boutique in California, and the rest is history. Her line is very popular on QVC and she has sold millions to date and has become one of the biggest names on QVC.

3 Jamie Foxx

Believe it or not, Jamie Foxx has done pretty well selling his Prive Revaux glasses on QVC. The glasses are high-quality and very modern and stylish reading glasses at an affordable price. Jamie had a hand in creating the readers, and he and his celebrity friends swear by them. He's also released the Prive Revaux Dynamic Duo Blue Light Readers to not only help you read on your tablet or smartphone but to also help block out the blue light from your electronics. The glasses are best sellers and very popular with QVC viewers.

2 Isaac Mizrahi

Fashion designer Isaac Mizrahi is one of the most popular celebrities to sell their products on QVC. Naturally, Isaac sells all things fashion from clothing to accessories and even jewelry. His line is so popular that he even has his own segment on QVC called Isaac Mizrahi Live! which airs on Fridays at 1 pm and 9 pm ET. There are hundreds of fashion choices for sale under his collection, and there's definitely something for everyone. His clothing has high ratings and many enjoy the variety of clothing options that he shares with the network.

1 Jessica Simpson

Since stepping away from music, Jessica Simpson has worked hard on creating her very own fashion empire. Jessica creates everything from clothing, to accessories, and even has her own line of shoes. Her Jessica Simpson line of shoes is very popular amongst fans and fashion lovers alike and has done very well whenever she sells her shoes on QVC. She has even sold her shoes on QVC and donated 80% of the profits to benefit breast cancer research. Whether she's selling them for charity or not, Jessica's shoes are definitely a hit on QVC.

Catherine Zeta-Jones

Catherine Zeta-Jones doesn't strike us as a celebrity who would be making a ton of money selling their products on QVC, but she's definitely one of them. Catherine sells her collection, Casa Zeta-Jones exclusively on QVC. The collection features luxury home products such as bedding and other decor. She is working on expanding her products, however, currently, you can purchase a number of different bedding sets as well as decor for your home on QVC. The designs are very elegant and scream luxury, offering you a look into some of the things that she loves for her own home.
