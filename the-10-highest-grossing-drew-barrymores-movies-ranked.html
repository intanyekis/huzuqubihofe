American actress, director, and producer Drew Barrymore has been in the spotlight for most of her life. The actress who began acting since she was five years old has amassed a massive $125 million net worth and is among the richest actresses in the world. As one of the most lovable actresses in Hollywood, it is not surprising that she had numerous box office movie success. In fact, Drew Barrymore's highest-grossing film ever was made when she was only six years old and her success in the movie industry has skyrocketed since. Among her highest grossing films are listed below.

Blended

The film Blended was released on May 23, 2014 and was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The movie directed by Frank Coraci has made $46,290,741 on domestic box office and grossed $127,990,741 on the worldwide box office. The romantic comedy film is about the recently divorced mom Lauren played by Drew Barrymore and a widowed dad Jim played by Adam Sandler who let their friends push them to date each other on a blind date. The date goes disastrously wrong and neither one of them wants to see each other again.

Music and Lyrics

The film Music and Lyrics was released on February 14, 2007 and was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The movie directed by Marc Lawrence has made $50,572,589 on domestic box office and grossed $146,196,885 on the worldwide box office. The movie is about the former music superstar Alex Fletcher played by Hugh Grant who used to be famous however now he is just playing nostalgia tours and county fairs. He finally scores a chance to have a major comeback when the reigning pop diva Cora Corman played by Haley Bennett asked him to write a song for her.

Beverly Hills Chihuahua

The film Beverly Hills Chihuahua was released on October 3, 2008 and was distributed by Walt Disney Pictures. The movie directed by Raja Gosnell has made $94,514,402 on domestic box office and grossed $154,218,168 on the world wide box office. The movie is about Chloe played by Drew Barrymore who is a pampered Chihuahua from Beverly Hills. She then gets a taste of the real world when she got lost in the tough part of Mexico.

Scream

The film Scream was released on December 20, 1996 and was distributed by Dimensions Film. The movie directed by Wes Craven has made $103,046,663 on the domestic box office and grossed $173,046,663 on the world wide box office. The movie is about the reinvented and revitalized version of the slasher-horror genre created by Wes Craven. The film manages to be clever, funny yet scary.

He's Just Not That Into You

The film He's Just Not That Into You was released on February 6, 2009 and was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The movie directed by Ken Kwapis has made $93,953,653 on the domestic box office and grossed about $181,053,657 on the worldwide box office. The movie is about the Baltimore based group of friends who are all in their 20s and 30s. Each of them tries to navigate their way through the many complexities of the modern relationships.

50 First Dates

The film 50 First Dates was released on February 13, 2004 and was released by Columbia Pictures. The movie directed by Peter Segal has made $120,776,832 domestic box office and grossed $196,320,329 on the world wide box office. The movie is about the playboy vet Henry played by Adam Sandler who sets his heart on romancing Lucy played by Drew Barrymore. Lucy has a short term memory loss so she cannot remember anything that happened the previous day.

Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle

The film Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle was released on June 27, 2003 and was released by Columbia Pictures. The movie directed by McG has made $100,814,328 on domestic box office and grossed $227,163,273 on the world wide box office. The movie is about the Angels played by Lucy Liu, Drew Barrymore and Cameron Diaz who are all ready to go undercover in order to retrieve two missing silver wedding bands that contains a valuable encrypted information.

Charlie's Angels

The film Charlie's Angels was released on November 3, 2000 and was released by Columbia Pictures. The movie directed by McG has made $125,305,545 on the domestic box office and grossed $259,736,090 on the world wide box office. The movie is about the trio of elite private investigators who are armed with the latest gadgets and tools as well as high-performance vehicles. These agents are also highly trained in martial arts techniques which will come handy when they are faced with threats and obstacles to their mission.

Batman Forever

The film Batman Forever was released on June 16, 1995 and was released by Warner Bros. Pictures. The movie directed by Joel Schumacher has made $184,031,112 on the domestic box office and $336,529,144 on the worldwide box office. The movie is about Batman played by Val Kilmer who are facing against the schizophrenic and horribly scarred villain Two Face played by Tommy Lee Jones and Riddler played Jim Carrey.

E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial

The film E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial was released on June 11, 1982 and was released by Universal Pictures. The movie directed by Steven Spielberg has made $435,110,554 on the domestic box office and $792,962,972 on the worldwide box office. The movie is about the gentle alien who became stranded on Earth and was later on discovered and befriended by the young boy named Elliott played by Henry Thomas.
