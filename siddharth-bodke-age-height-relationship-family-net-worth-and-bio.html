Bio of Siddharth Bodke

Indian actor Siddharth Bodke was born and raised in Naski. He was born in Nasik on November 30, 1995. He went to Day Care School in Nasik for his early education and then NBT Law College for his college education. During high school and college, he was involved in cultural activities and led the way in planning a number of festivals and cultural programs. This gave him a knack for acting, which became his passion later on.

So, once he was done with school, he went to Mumbai to follow his dreams. Siddharth Bodke has been getting a lot of praise for his role as David in “Drishyam 2,” which also stars Ajay Devgn and Tabu. But David wasn’t the part he was chosen for at first.

About the same thing, he told IANS: “I got a call from the casting agency for Vicky Sidana and tried out for another part in the movie. I was even chosen for that.
