What Is Bridgette Wilson’s Net Worth?

Bridgette Wilson-Sampras, an American model, singer, and actress, has a net worth of $25 million, with a large portion attributed to her husband, retired tennis star Pete Sampras. Wilson-Sampras is most recognized for her role as Veronica Vaughn in the 1995 comedy “Billy Madison.” She has appeared in over 30 films and television shows, including “Mortal Kombat,” “I Know What You Did Last Summer,” and “The Wedding Planner.” Wilson-Sampras also produced videos for “Michael Jackson: HIStory on Film – Volume II” and released two albums, “I Only Wanna Be with You” and “Gimme a Kiss.” Although she hasn’t appeared on-screen since 2008, she voiced Sonya Blade in the 2020 video game “Mortal Kombat 11.”

Bridgette Wilson’s Early Life

Bridgette Wilson was born in Gold Beach, Oregon in 1973. She attended Gold Beach High School where she played volleyball and won the Miss Teen USA pageant in 1990. She grew up with her mother, father, and sister Tracy.

Early Career

Bridgette Wilson made her acting debut in 1992 on the soap opera “Santa Barbara” and had a recurring role on “Saved by the Bell” as Ginger. She appeared in the film “Last Action Hero” in 1993 and guest-starred on “Murder, She Wrote.” In 1995, Wilson appeared in several films, including “Higher Learning,” “Mortal Kombat,” and “Billy Madison.” She also played Elsa Shivers in the hit horror movie “I Know What You Did Last Summer.”

Later Career

In 2000, Wilson appeared in the film “Beautiful” and began playing Bridget Deshiel on the Fox drama “The $treet.” She co-starred in “Just Visiting” and “The Wedding Planner” the following year. Wilson also performed on the Tommy Shane Steiner song “What We’re Gonna Do About It” in 2002 and appeared in several films, including “Shopgirl” in 2005. She guest-starred on various TV shows and served as a judge for the 2006 Miss Universe pageant. Wilson also appeared in an off-Broadway production of “The Rocky Horror Show.”

Bridgette Wilson’s Family Life

Bridgette Wilson married professional tennis player Pete Sampras on September 30, 2000. The couple welcomed their first child, son Christian Charles, on November 21, 2002. They were blessed with their second child, Ryan Nikolaos, on July 29, 2005.

Pete Sampras and Bridgette Wilson’s Impressive Los Angeles Properties

In 2001, Pete and Bridgette purchased a 10,000 square-foot mansion in Beverly Hills for $8.9 million. They sold this home to Max Mutchnick in 2008 for $17 million. Mutchnick sold the property to Adam Levine in 2018 for $34 million, who then sold it in April 2021 for $45 million.

The couple also bought a property in Brentwood for $5.6 million in 2009. They sold it for $7.25 million in 2017 after listing it for $8.9 million in 2016.

In 2014, they bought two properties in Bel-Air, one for $5.9 million and the other for just over $3.5 million. The latter was turned into a gym and man cave.

Pete and Bridgette built a 16,000 square-foot mansion on Lake Sherman in Thousand Oaks, California, which they listed for $25 million in March 2021. They reportedly sold the property in an off-market deal in late 2013 for a bit over $13 million.
