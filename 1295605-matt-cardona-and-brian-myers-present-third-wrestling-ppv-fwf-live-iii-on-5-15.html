Matt Cardona And Brian Myers Present Third Wrestling PPV FWF LIVE! III On 5/15

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  275 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

May 13, 2022 — Professional wrestlers Matt Cardona and Brian Myers are taking their podcast network to the next level with their third wrestling show under the Major Pod Network banner. The pay-per-view, called FWF LIVE! III, will be LIVE on Long Island, NY at All Sportz in Melville, NY at 100 Ruland Road.  Doors open at 3 for a two hour meet and greet and the show starts at 5pm.  The show will also stream LIVE on Fite.tv ( https://www.fite.tv/watch/fwf-live-iii/2pb9g/) for 19.99. FWF LIVE! III will feature wrestlers and personalities from the Major Wrestling Figure Podcast and FWF (Figure Wrestling Federation), including Cardona, Myers, producer “Smart” Mark Sterling, Nick Gage, Chelsea Green, Deonna Purazzo, Dirty Dango, Effy, VSK and more.  The show is available for pre-order now and tickets are available on majorwfpod.com.

About the Major Pod Network: Matt Cardona and Brian Myers’ Major Pod Network is home to six podcasts about the world of wrestling collecting and wrestling history,‎ including Matt Cardona’s MC! True Long Island Story. The network grew out of their flagship podcast, The Major Wrestling Figure Podcast, which is a weekly deep-dive into wrestling figure and memorabilia collecting. Since the podcast’s inception in 2018, the brand has grown into a community for wrestling figure collectors, and it has expanded beyond podcasting to host live shows, a thriving Patreon.com community of collectors, and now its first wrestling show.

For more information or interview requests, contact majorpodcast@gmail.com.
