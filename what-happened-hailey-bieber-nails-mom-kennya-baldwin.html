Did Hailey Bieber's Mom Dislike Her Acrylic Nails?

Similar to many celebrities, Hailey Bieber has been exploring new nail trends and designs. While Hailey tends to maintain a natural aesthetic with her hair and makeup, she reveals that she has always been fond of manicures and experimenting with different styles. "Since I was a teenager, I really loved long nails, I always did acrylics, and it drove my mom crazy because she's so not like that," she told Allure.

Hailey Bieber has embraced the trend of ultra-long nails. However, over the years, the 26-year-old has transitioned away from acrylic nails and now prefers using PolyGel to achieve the added length. "It's basically a flexible gel that you can do extensions with, but you don't have to use anything acrylic, and it's a lot healthier for your nails," she says.

What Is Hailey Bieber's Favorite Nail Polish?

Hailey Bieber's current favorite nail technique involves using chrome dust to achieve a unique, eye-catching look. She explains that her nail artist, Zola, applies a thin layer of color before rubbing chrome dust over it.

Hailey also enjoys experimenting with custom color combinations, such as creating a jelly-like effect by mixing a deep purple shade with a clear polish. Her current obsession with chrome has led her to pair the metallic sheen with a variety of polishes, including vibrant shades like fuchsia pink and neon yellow.

Hailey's Instagram account showcases her thoughtful and chic approach to nail designs.

What Are Other Things Hailey Bieber's Mom Influenced Her To Do?

Hailey Bieber's mother, Kennya Baldwin, and grandmother instilled a strong focus on skincare in her from a young age. The Rhode founder shared with Vogue that her disciplined beauty routine is credited to her upbringing. In the podcast, Hailey explained that her mother used to slather her with lotions and creams after every shower, and emphasized the importance of hydration for the skin.

As a result, Hailey was exposed to cleansing, moisturizing, and skincare early on, and these practices became integral parts of her routine from a young age.

Another habit she picked up from her mother was removing makeup before bed, a cardinal rule that she has never broken. Her skincare obsession has now come full circle with the launch of her skincare brand, Rhode and her mother and grandmother have become fans of her brand and love using its products.
