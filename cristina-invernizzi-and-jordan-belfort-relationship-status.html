Belfort's Past Marital Affairs

The real Wolf of the Wall Street, Jordan married three times and his first marriage was with Denise Lombardo, a real estate agent at Prudential Douglas Elliman Company. Moreover, the pair divorced in 1991, after living in a matrimonial relationship for six years.

After the separation, Belfort immediately exchanged his wedding vows with a British-American model, Nadine Caridi in 1991. The duo met at a party, where Nadine's ex-boyfriend, Alan Wilzig introduced Jordan.

The couple, however, separated and filed for divorce in the late-2004 after hearing the claims of domestic violence by Jordan due to drug addiction and extramarital affairs. Later, they settled down with a divorce settlement and the custody of their two children was given to Nadine in 2005.

Following the divorce with Nadine, Belfort tied the knot for the third time with an American businessperson, Anne Belfort in an intimate ceremony in 2008. Nevertheless, their conjugal relationship did not last long because of some misunderstanding and the marriage ended with another divorce settlement.
