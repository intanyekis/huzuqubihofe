Who is Shree Crooks? 

Born in April 2005, Shree Crooks is an American teen actress. The young actress made her acting debut when she was only seven years old and so far, her acting credit is 13.

How Much is the Net Worth of Shree Crooks?

As of 2019, Shree Crooks has a net worth of $600 thousand which she made from her career in acting. She played various roles in both movies and television series.

Crooks made her acting debut in 2012 from the tv series, The New Normal in which she played the role of Avery. Following that, she appeared in four episodes of another show Extant as a young Katie Sparks. 

Shree’s another series is Ray Donovan (2015) in which she portrayed Audrey in six episodes. In addition, she played various roles in other series like NCIS: Los Angeles. Moreover, her upcoming series Staged Dad is in post-production.

Her another 2017 movie The Glass Castle made a box office collection of $22 million. In this film, she worked alongside Brie Larson, Woody Harrelson, Naomi Watts, Eden Grace Redfield, Sadie Sink, and so on. Furthermore, she also played the role of little match girl in Little Match Girl (2018).
