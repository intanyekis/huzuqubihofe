Did Taylor Swift Save Brittany Mahomes' Tarnished Image?

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1135 words  |  Martina Birk

Highlights

  • Despite facing criticism and negativity, Brittany Mahomes seems to have found solace and support in her friendship with Taylor Swift.
  • The bond between Brittany Mahomes and Taylor Swift has garnered mixed reactions.
  • While some Patrick Mahomes fans remain skeptical about Brittany, her friendship with Taylor has brought a positive change in public perception.

Taylor Swift is enjoying a break from her 2023 Eras Tour, and while she keeps releasing new music like her version of her hit song "Cruel Summer," her love life is still making headlines. Thanks to Taylor's alleged relationship with Travis Kelce, the beloved singer has become friends with Patrick Mahomes's wife Brittany. Patrick and Brittany Mahomes live a nice lifestyle and, now, that includes being part of Taylor's circle.

Ever since Patrick and Brittany Mahomes began dating, his fanbase has said some pretty terrible things about her. It's rare to see a post online that praises Brittany instead of listing reasons why she is problematic. However, now that Brittany Mahomes is in the spotlight because of her friendship with Taylor Swift, does that mean that people feel differently about her?

Taylor Swift's Friendship With Patrick Mahomes' Wife Has Changed Her Public Image

When someone marries a football player or is in a long-term relationship with any public figure, they expect to be under a certain level of scrutiny. However, many would agree that people seem particularly mean about Brittany Mahomes, and Patrick's fans never stop talking about her.

Some people seem to be warming up to Brittany Mahomes because of her friendship with Taylor Swift. Page Six reported that the new pals had created a "BFF handshake" that had "a hip bump" at the Kansas City Chiefs/Los Angeles Chargers game on Sunday, October 21st, 2023. The two were sitting in the stands smiling and having fun along with Sterling Skyle, Patrick and Brittany's two-year-old daughter.

Brittany Mahomes and Kayla Nicole have been tight for years, but now that Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift could be dating, things seem to be different.

According to a headline from TMZ, the "special handshake gets mixed reviews." The publication reported that some people shared on X that this was proof that Brittany and Taylor are "besties" and that's a good thing. Another fan wrote "It's peak girlhood I love it so much." Some people seem to really enjoy seeing photos of the two together.

In a Reddit thread about Brittany Mahomes and Taylor Swift's handshake, several people shared that they like seeing the bond between the two.

Redditor @dbgurl7 wrote "I don’t know much about Brittany but I gather she’s had a huge amount of scrutiny on her and it’s not always positive—what I really appreciate about this friendship is Taylor got ahead of what would have been the inevitable 'Blonde vs Blonde' headlines and social media would have had a field day tearing either or both of them apart at even the whiff of tension. Instead they’re out here, living their best lives, watching their significant others kick a** and being able to support them."

Redditor @needopinioporfavor also commented, "I'm glad they have fun together!"

However, some people posted on X that the handshake was "cringe," which proves that some people still aren't big fans of Patrick Mahomes's wife.

Fans wonder if Brittany Mahomes was supportive when Patrick Mahomes hurt his leg in January 2023 and had to recover from this scary moment.

One Redditor replied, "Brittany popped a bottle of champagne on the crowd at arrowhead after the win. I’m not sure if the people below her cared but twitter didn’t like it."

Redditor @Seashelz asked in the FauxMoi SubReddit "Can we discuss Patrick Mahomes' fiancee and her lack of self-awareness." It seems that the champagne moment upset a lot of people, and it has been discussed in several Reddit threads, including this one. People say that Brittany never should have done that.

In general, there seem to be Patrick Mahomes fans who like his wife Brittany Mahomes and don't understand why others get so upset about her. Others still aren't sure about her, and it doesn't seem like her friendship with Taylor Swift has changed that fact.

There is a SubReddit called Brittany Matthews Snark where people post frequently about the problems that they have with Brittany. There has been a lot of activity in the SubReddit since Brittany and Taylor have become friends. Some say that they don't want to read about their friendship and others say that they aren't sure that they are actually pals.

Inside Brittany Mahomes And Taylor Swift's Friendship

With every day that passes, there seems to be another news story about the new relationship between Brittany Mahomes and Taylor Swift. The two seem to be growing closer by the minute, and they have been spending a lot of time together at games.

According to E! News, Brittany seems to be part of Taylor Swift's "squad" these days, which also features Sophie Turner and Blake Lively. There have been some dinners in New York City besides all the hang-outs in the stands at football games.

While the two haven't commented on their friendship, they seem to get along great. A source explained to Us Weekly,

"Brittany is thrilled to be building a genuine friendship with Taylor."

Although Brittany Mahomes hasn't talked publicly about being close with Taylor Swift, she has opened up about the fact that so many strangers talk negatively about her.

According to The Independent, in March 2023, Ari Shaffir, Mark Normand and Shane Gillis didn't have the nicest things to say about Brittany. Gillis called her "crazy" and "wild."

Brittany tweeted, “Something about grown men talking sh*t about someone’s wife is real weird. Actually grown a** men hating on women in general is pathetic."

While not everyone loves Brittany Mahomes, she seems to take the negative comments in stride, and she is still having fun and living her life the best way that she can. By this point, she must be used to hearing people talk about her. Unfortunately, this is an inevitable byproduct of marrying someone who is in the public eye. It's possible that Brittany and Taylor can relate to one another since they are both used to reading mean comments online.

Even if some of Patrick Mahomes's fans never accept Brittany, many people are glad to see her befriending Taylor Swift. It's hard to argue with the fact that the stories about Brittany and Taylor's friendship are a breath of fresh air since it's nice to read happy news.
