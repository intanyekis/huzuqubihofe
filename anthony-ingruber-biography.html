Who Is Anthony Ingruber?

Anthony Ingruber is a Dutch-Australian actor and impressionist. He is popularly known for playing the role of John Doe/Joker in the video game Batman: The Telltale Series. He is also known for his roles in The Age of Adaline and Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny.

Anthony Ingruber-Professional Life, Careers

Talking about his career, Anthony Ingruber has been active in the entertainment industry since 2006. He has worked in several movies and television series till now. He began his career in 2006 by appearing as a Policeman in the television show Sensing Murder which was created by David Baldock.

Moreover, he posted videos on YouTube that included a Han Solo impression in 2008. Anthony has more than 24k subscribers on his channel and has posted around 74 videos on his channel.

He played a minor role as a technician in James Cameron’s Avatar. He made his first appearance in a movie in 2010 by playing the role of Sean in the Disney TV movie Avalon High.

Furthermore, Anthony is pretty famous on the Internet for his impressions which helped to gain the role of Young William, the young version of Ford’s character, in the drama The Age of Adaline in 2015. In the same year, he went on to appear in Eyes Open Nevermore and Sincerely, Batman
