Shea McGee Net Worth, Design, Dream Home Makeover

The episodes of Dream Home Makeover would show Shea and her husband take over all kinds of projects from budget-friendly makeovers to full-blown extravagant home makeovers.

The duo work under their own interior design and e-commerce business company with the name Studio McGee.

In the trailer of the show, they said about helping clients with budgets from $20k to anywhere up to $2 million. Also, according to Netflix, the company hired 70 employees and dealt with clients all over the world.

This happened circa 2014 after Shea with a two-month-old baby thought on blossoming her “little” design business as she launched a website and got an Instagram account. “My business grew exponentially,” Shea recalled.

People from throughout Southern California, and even across the country, asked her to design their home. At the time, she had no idea how to handle the workload. But working with her husband was never on her mind. Until on a certain day, it clicked to her when Syd was searching for a new start-up to dive into. “I remember crying and asking, ‘Are you sure we can do this?”, she said. And the answer, as we can see it now, was surely a ‘Yes”.

The couple then sold their home, moved to Utah, and started Studio McGee in Salt Lake City.

Years later, as Syd and Shea McGee collectively boast a net worth of around $4-5 million, they have a dreamy new home in Utah. The place is no doubt every interior design lover’s dream. Much of the home is featured on its Instagram account.

Together, Syd and Shea have teamed up with Threshold for Target to launch their own home decor line, which is available everywhere, in case you are interested.

Shea & Syd McGee: Dating, Wedding, Kids

Shea McGee met Syd through her brother. The McGee siblings were moving home and Shea went along to help him, when Syd, who was a friend of her brother’s had come along.

In the couple’s book, Make Life Beautiful, Shea describes the moment as– “There Syd was on a skateboard, wearing the skinniest skinny jeans I had ever seen. We went on our first date the next night.”
